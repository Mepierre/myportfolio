About Me

Marsha Pierre is the founder and CEO of Signature Cocktails by Blaque, LLC and The Blaque Experience, a luxury source for cocktails and events. In her lifetime she’s been blessed to acquire many skills. Skills that have allowed her to be more confident in her journey as an entrepreneur. From the tender age of 16, Marsha always knew that one day she’d either be working for herself or for a company that would allow her to be very independent. In 2018, Marsha was given the opportunity to be apart of Wyncode’s Women In Technology Program. She is so excited about this new technology journey and can’t wait to leave her mark in this industry.”

My professional goal is to one day become a Business Mogul. I want to build diverse companies that focuses on one's talent versus social status or looks. My dream is to bridge Beauty, Fashion, Entertainment, Health and Technology all in one. I haven’t quite figured out exactly how I will put it all together to accomplish this goal but I am definitely working towards it.
